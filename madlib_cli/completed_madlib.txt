Make Me A Video Game!

I the green and green Sarah have ran Meg's green sister and plan to steal her green LaCroixs!

What are a elk and backpacking squirrel to do? Before you can help Chloe, you'll have to collect the bold lampshades and green ferris wheels that open up the 48 worlds connected to A Samra's Lair. There are 21 perfumes and 4783 marathons in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
